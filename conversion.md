### Conversion Rates:

I always find myself forgetting these, so here I wrote them:

#### Create:
- 1 W -> 1 su

#### Forge Energy:
- 1 FE/t -> 34 W
- 1 Wh -> 2 118 FE (2.118 kFE)
- 1 kWh -> 2.118 MFE
- 1 MWh -> 2.118 GFE

Keep in mind `FE/t` is different from `FE`: Think of `FE/t` as active power, currently flowing, and `FE` as the total energy.
`FE/t` is convertible to `Watts`, and `FE` is convertible to `Watt-hours`.

Also abandon the `1 FE` -> `1 J` conversion. This mod's conversion rate is different to ensure creates `su` to `Watts`
is 1 : 1, and `FE/t` -> `Watts` is based on FE Motors from other addons with the `su` -> `Watts` conversion.

#### Cheat Sheet:

- k - kilo - thousand
- M - Mega - Million
- G - Giga - Billion
- W - Watts
- su - Stress units
- FE - Forge Energy
- FE/t - Forge Energy per tick
- Wh - Watt hours
- kWh - thousand Watt hours
- MWh - million Watt Hours
